## Limitations and Exclusions

<v-click>

**Exclusions**
- Pre-existing conditions for 12 months
- Elective surgeries
- Dental procedures not due to injury

</v-click>

<v-click>

**Limitations**
- 30-day waiting period for sickness benefits
- No coverage for experimental treatments

</v-click>
