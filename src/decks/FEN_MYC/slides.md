## How MBR Plus Works

<v-clicks>

1. Enroll through **BWA**
2. Pay $25 pre-pay for primary care visits
3. Pay $50 pre-pay for specialist visits
4. Receive Explanation of Benefits **(EOB)**
5. Submit bills for repricing
6. **Lower Out-of-Pocket Expenses**

</v-clicks>
