## How Hospital Bill Reducer Works

<v-clicks>

1. Enroll through **Reliant Care**
2. Submit hospital bills
3. Review and negotiate charges
4. Receive adjusted bill **(HBR)**
5. Pay reduced amount
6. **Save on healthcare costs**
