## How Dental Rewards Work

<v-clicks>

1. Enroll through **Ameritas**
2. Visit a dental provider annually
3. Submit a claim under $500
4. Receive $250 in rewards **(DR)**
5. Use Ameritas network for bonuses
6. **Maximize annual benefits**

</v-clicks>
